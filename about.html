Our Story

Welcome to Luxe Hotel, where comfort meets elegance.

The story of the elegant Mbale Resort Hotel begins in the early 2000s, founded around 2002 by a group of indigenous private investors and spearheaded by businessmen like Drake Mugisa and James Wakadala.

Conceived as a premier luxury destination for Eastern Uganda, the hotel initially opened with a modest 19 rooms. However, its prime location against the backdrop of Mount Elgon and the stunning views of Wanale Hill quickly established it as a regional landmark.

A major milestone was the 2010 expansion with the opening of a new complex, significantly increasing its capacity to nearly 100 rooms and adding multiple conference and dining facilities. This solidified its role as the major luxury, business, and conferencing hub in the region.

Today, the Mbale Resort Hotel stands as a symbol of the region's development and a gateway for tourists exploring Eastern Uganda's treasures, including the nearby Sipi Falls and the Nyero Rock Paintings.
